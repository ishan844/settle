# Gopuff — Competitor Screenshot Analysis

## Overview
Gopuff is a convenience & alcohol delivery app. The screenshot shows the **Home screen** with a focus on deals, product browsing, and fast delivery.

---

## Color Usage
- **Primary palette**: Deep navy blue (`~#003366`) dominates the header, search bar background, and bottom navigation — creates a strong, trustworthy brand identity.
- **Accent colors**: Bright green (`~#00C853`) used for delivery time badge ("25-41mins") and the "5¢ sale" tags — draws attention to urgency and deals.
- **Secondary accents**: Red/coral (`~#FF3D00`) for the "Buy 4 Wines, Save 10%" CTA buttons — signals promotions aggressively.
- **White background**: The main content area uses a clean white background for product cards and banners, creating strong contrast against the dark header.
- **Category icons**: Use vibrant, multi-color circular thumbnails against a white backdrop.

## Typography
- **Header font**: Bold, sans-serif (likely a custom brand font). "gopuff" branding uses a lowercase, friendly wordmark.
- **Search bar text**: Medium-weight placeholder text set in a neutral gray.
- **Section headers**: "DEALS FOR ALL" — uppercase, bold, dark sans-serif. Strong hierarchy.
- **Product names**: Medium-weight, smaller body text. Readable but secondary to images.
- **Price text**: Standard weight, dark gray. Not overly emphasized — the deal badges do the heavy lifting.
- **CTA buttons**: Small, bold white text on red/coral pill-shaped buttons.

## Layout & Spacing
- **Vertical stacking**: Classic mobile feed layout — header → search → categories → promo banner → product grid → promotional banner → nav bar.
- **Category row**: Horizontal scroll with evenly spaced circular icons + labels beneath. ~5 visible with room to scroll.
- **Product cards**: Horizontal scrolling row, ~2.5 cards visible at a time (peek pattern encourages swiping). Card width roughly 40% of screen.
- **Generous padding**: Promo banner ("New here? Get 50% off") has comfortable internal padding with a clear CTA button on the right.
- **Bottom nav**: 5-tab navigation with icon + label, evenly distributed. Active tab (Home) highlighted in blue with the "go" brand icon.

## Design Patterns
- **Urgency indicators**: Delivery time badge with lightning bolt icon (⚡) in the top-left creates time pressure.
- **Deal badges**: Green "5¢ sale" starburst/badge overlaid on product images — a proven pattern for drawing the eye to promotions.
- **Peek/scroll pattern**: Partially visible cards on the right edge invite horizontal scrolling.
- **Promo banner**: Full-width card with illustration (shopping bag) + clear value proposition ("50% off your 1st order!") and a strong "Claim now!" CTA.
- **Search integration**: "Search gopuff + BevMo!" — co-branded search bar reinforces the expanded catalog.
- **Group ordering**: "gogroup" feature in top right — social/group ordering capability.
- **XL promo banner**: Full-bleed lifestyle photo banner at the bottom ("BIG VALUE. DELIVERED FAST.") with warehouse imagery — builds trust for bulk/value ordering.

## Spacing & Sizing Observations
- **Header zone**: ~120px tall including status bar, delivery info, and search bar.
- **Category icons**: ~60px diameter circles with ~16px spacing between them.
- **Product cards**: ~140px wide, ~220px tall including text below image.
- **Card corner radius**: Soft rounded corners (~12px) on product cards and promo banner.
- **Bottom nav height**: Standard ~50px with evenly distributed icons.

## Key Takeaways
1. **Deal-forward design** — nearly every element promotes a deal, discount, or value proposition.
2. **Dark header / light body** contrast creates a clear information hierarchy.
3. **Horizontal scrolling** is used heavily to maximize content density without overwhelming.
4. **Urgency and time** (delivery estimates, open hours) are prominently displayed.
5. **Bottom navigation** is standard 5-tab with clean iconography.
6. **Brand personality** feels bold, value-driven, and slightly playful (starburst badges, bright colors).
