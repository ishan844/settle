# Swiggy — Competitor Screenshot Analysis

## Overview
Swiggy is India's leading food delivery platform. The screenshot shows the **Home screen** with a multi-service hub (Food, Instamart, Dineout, Scenes) and heavy promotional content.

---

## Color Usage
- **Primary brand color**: Deep orange (`~#FF5200`) — used for the Swiggy logo, active tab highlights, and key CTAs ("ORDER NOW").
- **Header gradient**: Dark navy/indigo gradient (`~#1B1464` → `~#2D2B70`) in the top section — creates a premium, bold feel. The gradient fades into white as you scroll down.
- **Accent yellow/gold**: Used in promotional banners and deal badges for warmth and excitement.
- **White background**: Clean white for the main content feed below the hero section.
- **Green accents**: Stars on restaurant ratings use a bright green (`~#48C479`), consistent with trust/quality signaling.
- **Red discount badges**: Bold red/orange overlays on restaurant cards showing "65% OFF", "₹165 OFF" — high-contrast deal tags.
- **Tab filters**: Active tab ("MIN Rs. 100 OFF") uses orange text on a light orange background; inactive tab uses dark text on light gray.

## Typography
- **Location header**: Bold, white, large sans-serif for the location name. Subtitle address in lighter/smaller gray text.
- **Service icons**: Bold, uppercase labels beneath circular icon buttons ("FOOD", "INSTAMART", "DINEOUT", "SCENES").
- **Search bar**: Light gray placeholder text ("Search for 'Pizza'") — contextual and suggestive.
- **Promo banner**: Mix of bold display text ("Foodie Weekend") and smaller body text. "ORDER NOW" in a bold pill button.
- **Deal chips**: Mixed typography — some use stylized/custom fonts ("FEAST MODE", "FLASH DEALS") to create visual variety and brand personality.
- **Restaurant names**: Bold, dark, truncated with ellipsis for overflow ("CakeZone Patis...").
- **Meta text**: Small gray text for ratings, delivery time, and cuisine type.

## Layout & Spacing
- **Service hub**: 4 circular icon buttons in a row at the top — each with an icon, badge (e.g., "9 mins" on Instamart), and label. Evenly spaced.
- **Search bar**: Full-width with rounded corners, includes a microphone icon and a "VEG" toggle filter on the right — unique multi-function search.
- **Hero promo**: Full-width banner with food imagery on both sides, centered text, and a CTA button. Visually rich.
- **Deal chips row**: Horizontal scroll of 4+ small cards with icon + text. Each card is ~80px wide with rounded corners.
- **Filter tabs**: Two segmented tabs ("MIN Rs. 100 OFF" and "10 MINS DELIVERY") — horizontal toggle pattern.
- **Restaurant cards**: Horizontal scrolling row, ~2.5 cards visible. Each card includes: image (with overlaid discount badge + heart icon), name, rating, delivery time, and cuisine type.
- **Bottom nav**: 6-tab navigation (Food, Offer Zone, Bolt, Eatlist, Card, Reorder) — more tabs than typical, uses a "10 MIN" badge on "Bolt" tab.

## Design Patterns
- **Multi-service platform**: Top-level service selection (Food, Instamart, Dineout, Scenes) positions Swiggy as a super-app.
- **Badge overlays**: "9 mins" on Instamart icon, "10 MIN" on Bolt tab — time-based urgency integrated into navigation.
- **Discount-heavy**: Almost every visible element promotes a deal — discount badges on cards, promo banners, deal chips, filter tabs.
- **Heart/favorite icon**: Translucent heart overlay on restaurant cards — allows quick favoriting.
- **"AD" label**: Small "AD" tags on promoted restaurant cards — transparent ad disclosure.
- **Voice search**: Microphone icon in search bar — accessible input method.
- **VEG toggle**: Dietary filter directly in the search bar — smart contextual filtering unique to Indian market.
- **Gamified deal names**: "FEAST MODE", "FLASH DEALS", "Bolt⚡" — playful, branded deal categories that feel like game modes.

## Spacing & Sizing Observations
- **Header zone**: ~180px including status bar, location, service hub, and search bar.
- **Service icons**: ~60px diameter with ~24px spacing between centers.
- **Deal chip cards**: ~80px × 80px with ~8px rounding and ~8px gap between cards.
- **Restaurant cards**: ~150px wide, ~250px tall including text metadata.
- **Corner radii**: Consistent ~12-16px on cards, ~24px on pill buttons, fully rounded on avatar/service icons.
- **Bottom nav**: Slightly taller than standard (~60px) to accommodate 6 tabs.

## Key Takeaways
1. **Super-app positioning** — the service hub at the top establishes Swiggy as more than just food delivery.
2. **Extremely deal-driven** — every section of the screen promotes discounts, creating a sense of incredible value.
3. **Dark-to-light gradient** header creates depth and premium feel while allowing the content area to remain clean.
4. **Playful branding** — custom typography on deal chips ("FEAST MODE", "FLASH DEALS") adds personality and gamification.
5. **Dense information architecture** — more content per viewport than most competitors, relying on horizontal scroll to manage.
6. **Contextual search** — VEG toggle and voice search built directly into the search bar is innovative.
7. **6-tab bottom nav** is atypically wide — may cause usability issues on smaller screens but maximizes feature visibility.
